I'm a product designer and developer based in New York, originally from Portland. I studied interaction design at Parsons School of Design and spent years refining my craft at agencies and product studios across the city.

My work lives at the edge where visual design meets functional engineering. I care deeply about the spaces between things — the invisible architecture that makes a product feel inevitable once you've found it.
